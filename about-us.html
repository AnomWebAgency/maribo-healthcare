healthcare support

MARIBO HEALTHCARE LTD is a business established to provide both high quality domiciliary care. The service will work as part of an integrated health and social care model with the support of its co-founders who are qualified mental health professionals. The leading team have diverse experience within health and social care including mental health, which gives them an upper-hand and advantage in delivering a coordinated and cohesive service that is age inclusive for those that need to be cared for and supported to improve their life experiences and achieve their health and social needs whilst living independently in the community.

We are a comprehensive dominciliary care provider committed to delivering the highest quality of home care to different client group. We pledge to provide optimal home care and caregiver services that will allow our clients to enjoy a sense of safety and well-being while we are providing services for them.

All our caregivers go through a meticulous process of screening, interviews and examinations prior to employment. Caregivers are also regularly supervised and evaluated by management to guarantee that the highest service standards are met for our clients.

About Us

Maribo-Healthcare ltd is CQC regulated domiciliary care service. We aim to provide high-quality care to people in the comfort of their homes. Our team of highly trained professionals make sure that your loved ones receive the best care possible.
