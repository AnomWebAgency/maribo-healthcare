Maribo-healthcare provides a wide variety of treatments and services to speed recovery from illness or injury, help with the management of chronic conditions, make life easier for the elderly or handicapped, and to assist primary
caregivers with their responsibilities and ease their workload.

We focus solely on providing the highest quality of care. All services are provided in the home by our locally-based, highly trained, certified professionals.

Maribo-healthcare is committed to enhancing the lives of our senior citizens and ensuring that they achieve their maximum level of independence and self-actualization. We support environments that enable aging in place and opportunities to interact with others. We respect and revere our elders and believe that they have as much to offer us as we do them. With these beliefs in mind, we promise to deliver the very best care possible.

Our professionals are highly trained experts who respect and are devoted to what they do. They are more than just professionals; they are warm, caring men and women who often become their clients’ confidant.

Our staff and professionals understand that planning for the care of individuals is a measure that demands compassion and expertise.

What we offer but not limited to:

Support getting up, washed and dressed.

The Aides help your loved one with all types of personal care assistance. We assist your loved ones with hygiene, getting them up, taking a bath and dressing them. Our experts carefully match your loved one with a caregiver. A home health aide can take care of your loved one from the comfort of their own home.

Getting ready for bed

We provide the absolute highest level of personal care services for the elderly. Our certified staff members are well equipped to help seniors with daily activities that they cannot perform on their own, to help them maintain their quality of life as they age. Personal care providers from Maribo-healthcare limited will help your loved one with things like brushing their teeth, using the bathroom, and assisting them with getting ready for bed.

Companion Care

Caregivers can offer companionship for your elderly loved one, helping to keep them social and stimulated. All our caregivers are carefully selected during our hiring process. Our hiring process ensures that they are good companions for your family member. It is important for your loved one to continue to have a friend they can chat with day to day, eat meals with, or do other activities with. Put your mind at ease knowing that your relative has someone who cares about them taking care of them. Our caregivers go the extra mile with our clients, and we train every one of them to provide the best companionship possible. We pride ourselves on having the highest level of companion care for the elderly.

Medication prompts

Diseases like Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s or events like stroke can lead to language and speech problems in seniors. Our trained healthcare professionals help individuals suffering from these swallowing and speech disorders through speech pathology. We offer inclusive speech pathology services on an hourly, daily or live-in basis—whichever suits best as per client’s requirements.

Meal & Drink prompts

There are a variety of reasons that residents may need assistance with their meals, such as dementia or impaired cognition, weakness in hands (e.g. arthritis), poor vision, poor appetite, swallowing difficulties, or general weakness and fatigue. Whilst it is important to encourage residents to eat independently where possible, some residents will require assistance with their meals. The level of assistance that residents will need can vary, and may range from Supervision, prompting and encouragement; Setting up the meal (removing lids, placing cutlery, pouring drinks; Cutting up the meal; Providing full feeding assistance to the patient.

Light housework, laundry and ironing

Light housekeeping includes tasks such as dusting open surfaces; sweeping and mopping floors (damp mops – small areas and hard surface floors); vacuuming around furniture; laundry & ironing; wiping down counters; washing and putting dishes away; cleaning bathrooms and kitchens; taking out the trash; changing sheets and making the bed; and organizing drawers, shelves, and closets. Light housekeeping is meant to be limited to the areas used by the client such as the bathroom, kitchen, bedroom, and living room. Light housekeeping is offered as one part of an overall Service Plan to a senior to keep him or her safe and independent in their home environment.

Shopping services

As we get older some day-to-day tasks can become more challenging. This may be because of reduced mobility and frailty which restrict our independence.The carer will also facilitate all the social aspects to help with shopping services for the elderly. Our carers will help your loved one plan their shopping, so they have what they need, as and when they wish. This means your loved one can continue to enjoy life as they always have.

Collecting Prescriptions

This is where our medical home aide workers intervene, working constantly to improve the quality of our patients’ lives. These trained and certified professionals will help in collecting prescriptions for the various patients conditions as well as help them take the medicine adequately. They then work directly with the patient and, if necessary, the other health care professionals assigned to the case, to build the tools and techniques through which the patient recovers.
